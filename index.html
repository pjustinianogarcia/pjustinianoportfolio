About Me

I'm a Web Developer from Puerto Rico and hold a bachelor's degree in Hispanic Literature, but I've always had a strong interest in technology. My journey into web development began somewhat by accident—I took a Coursera course in Data Analytics, where I gained a lot of experience in Excel. However, the part I enjoyed most was the brief introduction to SQL. I found it exciting to write code, hit "run," and watch it work. That experience sparked my curiosity, and I started researching different programming languages and their applications. Web development stood out to me for its blend of creativity and visual impact, so I decided to join Career Foundry’s Full Stack Web Development program. There, I learned HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React, diving fully into the world of coding and building interactive applications. I'm a curious, creative problem-solver who loves the art of debugging and finding solutions. My passion for learning keeps me growing, and I approach every challenge with optimism and a positive outlook. Web development is my ideal field, combining my love for creativity, technical challenges, and the constant opportunity to learn and improve. With each project, I get to combine my curiosity, creativity, and determination to create something impactful.
